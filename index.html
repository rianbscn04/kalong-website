How Kalong Works

1. Book Your Ride

Open the app, enter your destination, and choose your preferred Kalong rider. Available 24/7 across major cities.

2. Meet Your Rider

Your verified Kalong rider arrives with secure "nakakalong" seating. Comfortable for 2 passengers plus luggage.

3. Ride Safe & Arrive Happy

Enjoy GPS-tracked journey with live updates. Pay securely through the app upon arrival.
